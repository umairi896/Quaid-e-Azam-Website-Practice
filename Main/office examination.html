Controller of Examinations

The Controller of Examinations is the whole-time officer of the University, appointed by the Syndicate. Quaid-i-Azam University has a long history of bright tradition of excellence in the field of Social, Biological, Physical, life and Medical Sciences. The Examination office of the University works under the supervision of the Controller of Examinations. The office of the controller of examinations is an integral component of the University administration.

The Controller of Examinations is responsible for all matters connected with the conduct of Examinations, Secrecy and degrees. Currently, Syed Muhammad Aqil Gillani is the Controller of Examination of Quaid-i-Azam University. There are three sections under the supervision of Controller of Examinations.

  • Examination Section
  • Comduct Section
  • Secrey Section
