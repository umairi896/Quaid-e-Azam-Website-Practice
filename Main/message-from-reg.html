QAU >> Message from Registrar

The two fold function of executing programmes with adequate planning, and academic assessment monitoring with feedback, are the prime tasks of the University management. We, as the custodian of such a system at the Quaid-i-Azam University, ensure its smooth operations and timely implementation by continuous improvement and quality enhancement. Our field experts implement all programmes with a vigilant eye on quality improvement and service.

The faculty and students is a valuable asset for us. We keep our doors open to listen to them as they are a direct source of information and feedback.

The University, therefore, while targeting its goals, endeavours to build a close relationship of troika comprising all stake holders. Such an approach helps fill the gaps and establishes a close coordination vital for efficient working. Building a link means establishing a stronger institution and a system for which we all stand for. So, for your best education, we look forward to sharing your feedback to make our system strong.

We assure you of our best service and cooperation for all times
